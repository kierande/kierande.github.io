Your website should be an investment, not an expense.

If you’ve commissioned design before you’ll be familiar with the scenario. A designer comes in, they talk a lot about fonts, show you some pretty pictures, talk about some projects they’ve worked with in the past and then talk about how lovely they’ll make your website look.

If you ask me, that’s not how it should be.

A designer should be asking you about your business. They should be asking you what pains you suffer - what problems you need solved to actually make a difference to your bottom line. They should be using great design - and the right tools - to make a measurable difference to the success of a business.

In my opinion, if a designer is talking more than they are listening, you’re with the wrong designer.
